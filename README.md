Neuronova is not another LLM project.
Its long-term goal is to go beyond current large language models by building a neuro-cognitive mathematical framework capable of representing meaning, scenes, operators, and linguistic structure with far greater interpretability and efficiency.

This prototype is part of a broader plan to develop a next-generation AI architecture, moving away from massive statistical black boxes and toward a system grounded in cognition, geometry and structured semantic dynamics.
